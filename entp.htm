Ellie Needs to Pee!

Ellie Needs to Pee is a short, lighthearted pachinko-style puzzle game. It follows the story of Ellie, a timid trans girl who's out shopping and really needs to pee, but feels way too anxious to use the public restroom.

Finish Ellie's shopping list by searching the aisles and playing pachinko games before time runs out!

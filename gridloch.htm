Gridloch

A short, musical puzzle game about Nessie's daughter, Nesselyn. Jr.

Move in time with the beat to help Nesselyn Jr. navigate her way through four worlds:

  • Ocean
  • River
  • Arctic
  • Space

...All while avoiding nasty enemies along the way!

Featuring adorable art and melodic tunes by Lite.
